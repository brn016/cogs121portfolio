Why did we come up with the Idea Development Tool Kit?

After working in so many different project teams, we wanted to create a more organized method for teams to collect their thoughts. We also wanted to allow teams to be able to revisit their inital inspirations and ideas down the line if they choose to iterate on their original ideas.
