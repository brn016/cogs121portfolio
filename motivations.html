Why did we come up with the Idea Development Tool Kit?

After working in so many different project teams, we wanted to create a more organized method for teams to collect their thoughts. We also wanted to allow teams to be able to revisit their inital inspirations and ideas down the line if they choose to iterate on their original ideas.

Many times, when teams are coming up with ideas, every individual keeps a pool of ideas in their minds, while only sharing the ones they deem are the best. We wanted to create an organized pool of mental chaos for all team members to dive into.

Centralized Pool of Ideas

Discussions over the ideas brought forth can often be difficult to keep organized, and difficult to allow everyone to have a say. Creating a digital forum for people to ask questions and post comments to any idea will let everyone be heard.

Making Every Voice Matter

There comes a time in the project where a team might want to revisit their original ideas.  A digital system is an efficient way to keep track of the ideas that motivated you to begin with and it won't be lost somewhere in your team's workspace.
