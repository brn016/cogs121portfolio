Prototype 1

Idea Submission

Our idea submission form allows team members to submit ideas to a centralized document. Ideas can take the form of text, images, links to videos, music. If a picture is worth a thousand words, what about a video? Songs? 

Review and Voting

Once all ideas are in, they appear in this sheet. On this sheet, users can upvote on ideas they like, or add comments to the right of the ideas. All users can see all comments. After this, the team would discuss the ideas with the most interactions (likes and comments).

Prototype 2

Idea Submission

Similar to the first prototype, the first step in using the product is to submit your ideas. Submissions can be text, links, images, anything that gives you inspiration for a problem.

Commenting and Asking Questions

Iterating on the last prototype, we made it much more streamlined to ask questions, or leave comments on other ideas. These questions and comments will be adressed in the next phase.

Review and Discussion

This sheet takes all the comments and questions and creates a list of topics to discuss verbally in a co-located space. Ideas with the most interactions under them might be areas of interest to further discuss.

Voting

After discussion the various ideas and their comments, teams can vote on the ideas they liked the most, or ideas they want to research and discuss in more depth moving forward.

Final Results

Here, we visualize the voting results by darkening the idea with the most votes. Lighter colors indicates less support for that idea. Sometimes, it might be best to pick just one idea, or your team can research further on the top ideas!
